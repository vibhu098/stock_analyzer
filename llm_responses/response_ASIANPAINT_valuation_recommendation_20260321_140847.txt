<h3>1. Valuation Framework Overview</h3>
<p>We employ a multi-method valuation approach — <strong>P/E Relative Valuation</strong>, <strong>Price-to-Book (P/B)</strong>, <strong>EV/EBITDA</strong>, <strong>Dividend Discount / FCF Proxy (DCF)</strong>, and <strong>Peer Comparison</strong> — to triangulate a fair value range for Asian Paints. Given the company's premium brand positioning, near-monopolistic domestic decorative paints market share (~53%), and consistent cash generation, a premium multiple is historically justified, though current competitive headwinds warrant scrutiny.</p>
