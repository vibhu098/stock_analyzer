<h2>Company Overview: Adani Enterprises Limited (ADANIENT)</h2>

<h3>1. Company Introduction</h3>

<p>
  <strong>Adani Enterprises Limited (NSE: ADANIENT)</strong> is the flagship holding and incubation entity of the Adani Group, one of India's largest and most diversified conglomerate groups. Headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, the company serves as the primary business incubator for the Adani Group, nurturing and scaling new ventures across multiple high-growth sectors before eventually listing them as independent entities. This unique incubation model has historically given birth to marquee group companies including Adani Ports, Adani Green Energy, Adani Total Gas, and Adani Transmission.
</p>

<p>
  The company operates across a broad spectrum of sectors including <strong>integrated resources management (IRM), mining services, airports, roads, data centers, solar manufacturing, defence, and agri-logistics</strong>. Its diversified business model straddles both asset-heavy infrastructure plays and asset-light trading/services businesses, making it a complex but strategically important entity within the Indian equity landscape. Adani Enterprises is classified within the <strong>Infrastructure &amp; Diversified Conglomerate</strong> sector and is a constituent of the <strong>NIFTY 50</strong> index, reflecting its systemic importance to the Indian economy.
</p>

<h4>Primary Business Segments</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Integrated Resources Management (IRM):</strong> Coal trading and logistics, forming the historical revenue backbone of the company, involving sourcing, logistics, and supply of thermal coal to domestic power utilities.</li>
  <li><strong>Mining Services:</strong> Mine development and operations (MDO) contracts, including Gondwana coalfields — one of the largest mining contracts awarded in India.</li>
  <li><strong>New Energy Ecosystem:</strong> Solar PV module manufacturing under the Adani Solar brand, positioning the company at the forefront of India's energy transition agenda.</li>
  <li><strong>Airports:</strong> Management and development of seven major airports across India (Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Lucknow, Mangaluru, Jaipur, Guwahati, and Thiruvananthapuram) through Adani Airport Holdings.</li>
  <li><strong>Roads &amp; Infrastructure:</strong> Execution of national highway projects under the HAM (Hybrid Annuity Model) and BOT frameworks.</li>
  <li><strong>Data Centers &amp; Digital Infrastructure:</strong> AdaniConneX, a joint venture targeting hyperscale data center capacity across major Indian cities.</li>
  <li><strong>Defence &amp; Aerospace:</strong> Manufacturing of small arms, ammunition, and aerospace components through strategic partnerships.</li>
  <li><strong>Agri-Logistics &amp; FMCG:</strong> Grain silos, food processing, and edible oil businesses under the Adani Wilmar ecosystem (associate entity).</li>
</ul>
