<h4>1b. Price-to-Book (P/B) Valuation — ROE-Justified Approach</h4>

<p>The Price-to-Book methodology is particularly relevant for Asian Paints given its asset-light model, strong brand intangibles, and consistently high Return on Equity (ROE). The Gordon Growth Model-derived P/B formula — P/B = ROE × (1 – g/ROE) / (Ke – g) — provides a theoretically grounded anchor for the justified multiple. Asian Paints' ROE has historically been in the range of 28–35%, significantly above its cost of equity, which justifies a substantial P/B premium.</p>
