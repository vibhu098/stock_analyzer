<h4>Investment Rationale:</h4>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Structural incubation value creation at inflection point:</strong> AEL's incubation pipeline — green hydrogen (ANIL targeting 1 MMTPA by 2030), Adani New Industries, Adani Airport Holdings, and Adani Road Transport — represents a combined enterprise value that is only partially reflected in the current market price. The SOTP analysis assigns ₹2,890/share in fair value, implying a 21.2% discount to intrinsic worth at the current price of ₹2,385.</li>
  <li><strong>Revenue and earnings trajectory strongly positive:</strong> Consolidated revenues grew from ₹44,268 crore in FY20 to ₹1,00,775 crore in FY24 — a 5-year CAGR of ~17.8%. FY26E EPS of ₹45.1 implies a 32% forward earnings CAGR from FY24 EPS of ₹25.8, driven by IRM margin recovery, airport EBITDA scaling, and reduced losses in nascent businesses.</li>
  <li><strong>Airport monetization and IRM business provide near-term earnings visibility:</strong> Adani Airport Holdings operates 7 airports with passenger traffic recovering to pre-COVID levels and growing. AAHL's EBITDA is expected to cross ₹3,500 crore in FY26E. Simultaneously, IRM (Integrated Resource Management) — AEL's largest revenue segment — benefits from commodity cycle normalization and long-term supply contracts.</li>
  <li><strong>Key risk — leverage and governance overhang:</strong> Net debt at the consolidated group level remains elevated at approximately ₹2.3 lakh crore (Adani Group). The Hindenburg Research allegations (January 2023), though partially rebutted by SEBI's investigation closure, continue to weigh on institutional sentiment. Any recurrence of governance concerns or credit rating downgrades could materially impair the stock. This risk is the primary constraint on upgrading conviction to HIGH.</li>
  <li><strong>Capex cycle peak provides medium-term free cash flow improvement catalyst:</strong> AEL's capex intensity is expected to moderate post-FY26 as major projects (green hydrogen, airport infrastructure, road projects) transition to operational phases. FCF yield, currently negative or near-zero, is projected to turn meaningfully positive by FY27, which could trigger a re-rating of the stock toward growth-oriented infrastructure peers.</li>
</ol>
