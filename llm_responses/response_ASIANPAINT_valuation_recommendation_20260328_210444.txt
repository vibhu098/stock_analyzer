  <li><strong>Competitive disruption is a real risk:</strong> Birla Opus's ₹10,000 Cr+ capex commitment and aggressive pricing in premium segments poses a credible long-term threat to volume share and pricing power — a structural overhang not yet fully priced in.</li>
</ul>
