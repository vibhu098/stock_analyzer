<h2>Asian Paints Limited (ASIANPAINT) — Company Overview</h2>

<h3>Business Description</h3>
<p>
  Asian Paints Limited is <strong>India's largest paint and coatings company</strong> and ranks among the top ten decorative coatings companies globally. Founded in 1942 and headquartered in Mumbai, the company manufactures and sells a wide range of <strong>decorative paints, industrial coatings, adhesives, waterproofing solutions, and home décor products</strong>. Its flagship brands include Asian Paints, Berger (international), Apcolite, Tractor Emulsion, and Royale. The company has expanded into adjacent categories such as bath fittings (Ess Ess), modular kitchens (Sleek), and waterproofing, positioning itself as an integrated <em>home improvement and décor solutions provider</em>.
</p>
<p>
  Asian Paints operates across <strong>15+ countries</strong> with manufacturing facilities in India and abroad. Domestically, it commands an estimated <strong>~50–55% market share</strong> in the decorative paints segment, supported by an extensive distribution network of over 70,000 dealers. The company benefits from strong brand equity, pricing power, and a deeply entrenched distribution moat.
</p>
