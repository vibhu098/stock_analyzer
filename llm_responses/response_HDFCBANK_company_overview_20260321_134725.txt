<h2>Company Overview: HDFC Bank Limited (HDFCBANK)</h2>

<h3>1. Company Introduction</h3>

<p>
  <strong>HDFC Bank Limited</strong> is India's largest private sector bank by assets and market capitalisation, listed on the NSE and BSE under the ticker symbol <strong>HDFCBANK</strong>. Headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, the bank operates within the <strong>Banking, Financial Services &amp; Insurance (BFSI)</strong> sector and is a constituent of the benchmark <strong>NIFTY 50</strong> index.
</p>

<p>
  In a landmark consolidation within Indian financial services, HDFC Bank completed the merger with its parent entity, <strong>HDFC Limited</strong> (Housing Development Finance Corporation), effective <strong>July 1, 2023</strong>. This transformative merger significantly expanded the bank's balance sheet, loan book, and deposit franchise, making it one of the largest financial institutions in Asia by total assets. The merger is reflected in the notable step-up in balance sheet size from FY23 to FY24, with total assets rising from approximately ₹24.7 lakh crore to ₹36.2 lakh crore.
</p>

<h4>Primary Business Activities</h4>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Retail Banking:</strong> Savings and current accounts, fixed and recurring deposits, personal loans, auto loans, credit cards, home loans, and wealth management services catering to individual customers.</li>
  <li><strong>Wholesale/Corporate Banking:</strong> Working capital finance, term loans, trade finance, cash management, and treasury services for large corporates, SMEs, and institutional clients.</li>
  <li><strong>Treasury Operations:</strong> Management of investment portfolios (government securities, corporate bonds, equities), foreign exchange, and derivatives, contributing significantly to non-interest income.</li>
  <li><strong>Housing Finance (Post-Merger):</strong> Following the absorption of HDFC Limited, mortgage and housing finance now constitutes a material component of the loan book, diversifying the credit portfolio beyond traditional banking products.</li>
  <li><strong>Digital Banking:</strong> HDFC Bank operates one of India's most advanced digital banking platforms, with significant investments in mobile banking, internet banking, and API-led financial services infrastructure.</li>
</ul>
